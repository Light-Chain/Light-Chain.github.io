What is a LightChain?

The open internet for everyone hosted by network of peer2peer nodes. The quantum leap of connectivity by wirelessly flowing light realtime. Channeling light through relays on pulses of light & radiowaves to achieve wireles data transfer.

Light Technology

Interconnecting internet of things by wireless transmitting data across pulsations of light. Reflecting light through relays embedded in QR stickers, cameras, solar panels & light sensors. Light technology will allow data to flow seemlessly across the waves of light.

The Internet of Things

The protocol is built for wireless IoT of the future. Transmitting bits of data across the light spectrums creates a large bandwidth capacity. Allow instant public internet access embedded into roadways, streetlamps, cars, and even bicycles!

Mine LightChain tokens!

Cryptographic LightChain tokens are rewarded for hosting the decentrailized internet protocol. Individual users can utilize the blockchain network, and get rewards for the amount of information your able to transfer through all your devices.
